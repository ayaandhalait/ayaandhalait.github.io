Frequently Asked Questions

Learn more about me, my work, and my projects.

Who are you?

I'm Ayaan Dhalait, a student developer and the Chief Technology Officer (CTO) at the Student Initiative Board (SIB). I build games, VR simulations, and interactive projects using Python, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

What is SIB?

SIB is a student-led organization focused on technology and innovation. I serve as the CTO and Head of the SWC, helping guide technical projects and mentoring student teams within the organization.

What kind of projects do you work on?

I work on Python-based games, VR and 3D simulations, and web development projects using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Where can I see your work?

You can see my work on my homepage, the articles and blogs section, and the App Store page where I showcase my apps and games.

How can I learn from your work?

You can explore my articles, blogs, and projects to understand how I build games, VR simulations, and Python projects. These provide tutorials, explanations, and code snippets for learners.

Are you open for collaborations or work?

I am not currently open for collaborations or work. This site is primarily to share my projects, articles, and tutorials for learning and reference.

What technologies do you use?

I mainly use Python for games and VR simulations, and HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for web development projects.

How is your work documented?

Most of my projects are documented online with tutorials, code examples, and visual demonstrations to help others understand how I create my work.
